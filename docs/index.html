CycleSafe

ride right, ride bright





Safety is our number one priority, is it yours?

CycleSafe is a semi-automatic bike light safety system that aims to protect all of its users from the dangers of biking at night. Our product provides a diversity of features in order to light up your bike so that cars on the road have no excuse for not seeing you and you can ensure that you have a safe ride at night.

flash_on

Laser Lanes

Our product is equipped with laser lanes to communicate to cars what space YOU need to ride. The laser feature is engaged right when you turn on the system and turns off when you press the power button, avoiding accidentally shutting off the system while your riding to ensure the safest ride.

directions

Turn Signals

The days are over when your turning or changing lanes and your worrying about a car noticing. With our turn signal technology, let cars clearly know when you want to move so they can be aware of you and your next move.

Car Sensors

Our car sensor technology is what completes our ultra safe technology. No need to check to ensure that a car is not next to you when you want to turn and no more worrying how close a car is. With our ultrasonic sensor technology, CycleSafe will warn you when a car is just too close for comfort.
